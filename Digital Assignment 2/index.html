Revenge on the Evil Twin

  • High Concept: Your evil twin is out to get you... RUN!
  • Theme: Revenge story (your evil twins wants to get revenge on you)
  • Mandated Variety: I used keyboard keys (W, A, S, and D for up, left, down, and right). The buildings spawn in different positions each game. The evil twin runs in semi random ways to get to you. This is an action game.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the players to experience an adrenaline rush trying to get away from the evil twin who gets faster over time.
