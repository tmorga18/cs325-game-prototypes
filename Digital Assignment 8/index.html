Werebear Attack

  • High Concept: Try to keep your score as close to zero before you're out of turns, your opponent will be doing the same thing!
  • Theme: FPS/Tactics game.
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse clicking.
  • Player Experience Goals: Competitiveness.
  • Gameplay: Decide which tile to eat up. The sign will randomly change each round... it will either add the number to your score or subtract it.
  • Prototype Goal:
  • Features: An instantiated grid which the player can move his werebear around on. There is a display, which tells you which grid tile you have selected. Ai that chooses a grid tile randomly. Both of your scores are adjusted automatically.
  • Setting/Premise: A grid.
  • Story: You are a werebear out in the wild and you encounter another werebear. Who will survive the encounter?
  • Target Audience: Everyone!
  • Play Time: 30 seconds to a minute.
  • Strategies: Think ahead about what your opponent is going to do and counter him.
