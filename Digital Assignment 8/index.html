Werebear Attack

  • High Concept: Move your werebear around the grid and queue up moves to attack your opponent.
  • Theme: FPS/Tactics game.
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse clicking.
  • Player Experience Goals: Competitiveness.
  • Gameplay: When it is your turn, queue up some actions and then you and your opponent play at the same time. Whoever kills the other first wins!
  • Prototype Goal: Got the grid and player movement semi-working, need to implement attacking and animations.
  • Features: An instantiated grid which the player can move his werebear around on. There is a display, which tells you which grid tile you have selected.
  • Setting/Premise: A grid.
  • Story: You are a werebear out in the wild and you encounter another werebear. Who will survive the encounter?
  • Target Audience: Everyone!
  • Play Time: 30 seconds to a minute.
  • Strategies: Think ahead about what your opponent is going to do and counter him.
