Pokemon Knock Off

  • High Concept: Collect as a team of Pokemon to start your journey!
  • Theme: I was aiming for the Preservationist theme. At first I wanted the character to protect the Pokemon in a sort of safari sanctuary, however I got a little offtrack and just remade Pokemon.
  • Mandated Variety: Input is direct using the WASD keys and it has a clickable button to start the game. It has a little bit of randomness to decide which song to play during the game and also to decide which way the pokemon will face. It is a top down adventure game based on the Gameboy game, Pokemon.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the players to experience a sense of adventure and I want them to explore the world and to enjoy the amazing assets I found online. I took a lot of time to implement them and to build the world.
  • Gameplay: The player can move in four directions. When they touch a Pokemon, the pokemon follows them around the map. This game was more about the polish of the looks and feel of the game rather than the gameplay. The goal of the game is to gather all of the Pokemon in the game (by walking up and colliding with them) and get them to follow you. Once you have gathered up a team, your friend will meet you and once you go collide with him, you can start your adventure!
  • Prototype Goal: I focused a lot on creating a working Tilemap that would visually draw in the player.
  • Features: I spent a lot of time looking for the right assets and tweaking them in Photoshop to look and feel just right. I also spent a lot of time creating Tile animations in Tiled, but it turns out that Phaser does not implement Tile animations. The player and the Pokemon can move behind buildings using depth sort. I also have a loading screen, a main menu with a start button and intro music, an intro screen, and a game over screen. There is a lot of music too, which will randomly play a song each time you start over. The pokemon also randomly look in different directions and when you touch them, they play a "happy" animation. They will also start to follow you. For the intro screen, there is a timer that cycles through a string array and will consecutively narrate text to the player. Then, it will prompt for you your name and Professor Oak will greet the player and send them on an adventure!
  • Setting/Premise: The game takes place in the world of Pokemon, in a new town that I created. It is surrounded by mountains, forests, a stream, and the ocean. There are a few houses and shops in the town.
  • Story: The story is the same in every Pokemon game. You start out as a young person who is just coming of age and is ready to head out and see the world and capture all the Pokemon.
  • Target Audience: I'm trying to reach all audiences, but probably the younger generations and anyone who enjoys exploring new worlds.
  • Play Time: Right now, it takes just a few minutes but I'm hoping to expand on this game and create more levels where the player can explore and find new pokemon. I would also (maybe) like to implement a battle system for catching Pokemon.
  • Strategies: There are no strategies right now since it's more about enjoying the setting.
